About the Match

The Barça vs Madrid — Community Clash is more than just a friendly game. It's an opportunity to bring our community together on the first day of the new year. Fans of both legendary clubs — FC Barcelona and Real Madrid — will unite to celebrate sportsmanship, unity, and passion for football.

The match aims to strengthen bonds, promote healthy rivalry, and create lasting memories. Each player registers under their preferred club, builds the squad, and contributes to making this event successful through their participation.
